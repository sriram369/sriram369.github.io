The Problem

The Accessibility
Gap in Navigation

  • Standard apps are built for mainstream users — not the 67M Americans with disabilities
  • They miss micro-barriers: cracked pavement, missing curb cuts, low-contrast signs, temporary obstructions
  • GPS fails entirely indoors — where accessible paths matter most
  • The result: limited independence, daily risk, and a persistent digital divide

Technology

Sensor Fusion Engine

90%+ Environmental Accuracy
🛰️
GPS
Provides macro-level location for initial route planning — sets the system's geographic context.
📡
LiDAR
Emits laser pulses to generate a real-time 3D depth map — enabling precise obstacle and elevation detection.
👁️
Computer Vision AI
Trained ML model recognizes objects, reads signs, and identifies safe pedestrian pathways from the camera feed.
📐
IMU Sensors
Accelerometer & gyroscope track orientation and micro-movements to refine position and analyze surface quality.
Redesigned Process

How AuraPath Works

01

Voice Initiation

"Hey AuraPath, take me to Gilman Hall" — simple, natural, hands-free command.

02

Dynamic Route Planning

System calculates a route based on accessibility data — not just distance, but actual real-world passability.

03

Continuous Environmental Scanning

Sensor Fusion Engine actively scans the path ahead, detecting obstacles traditional maps cannot see.

04

Adaptive Guidance

Descriptive audio and haptic alerts guide the user around obstructions, redesigning the journey in real-time.

05

Feedback Loop

Users rate route accessibility on arrival — feeding data back to improve the system for every future user.

Risk Assessment

Risks & Mitigations

⚙️  Hardware
MVP requires iPhone LiDAR. We focus iOS-first, then develop Android as LiDAR becomes standard on those devices.
🔒  Data Privacy
On-device AI processing, end-to-end encryption, and transparent data policies ensure GDPR compliance and user trust.
📣  User Adoption
Partner with trusted organizations like the National Federation of the Blind and universities to build credibility and drive awareness.
🏢  Competition
Move fast, patent core sensor fusion technology, and build a defensible brand focused entirely on accessibility.
